Battle of the droids

Who will play on behalf of the First Order and help BB-9E to stop and disable the plucky resistance droid?

Who will play on behalf of the resistance and help BB-8 throw off BB-9E so he can find a means of rescuing Finn and Rose from the vile First Order?

BB-8 and BB-9E are logged into the main system of Snoke’s flagship, the Supremacy, vying to gain control. Who will come out on top? Will it be a victory for the Resistance or will the First Order maintain control?

How to play

  1. Decide who is going to be who
  2. BB-8 you are trying to connect up the green wires and BB-9E you are trying to connect up the red wires.
  3. Click on the puzzle elements to rotate them, trying to correctly connect up your wire.
  4. You will take it in turns to try and win the puzzle with each player getting just ??seconds before your turn ends.
  5. The winner is the first one to correctly connect their wire.

Sounds easy? That is until you watch the opposition undoing all your hard work!
